Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026
TLDR — This extension runs entirely on your device. We collect zero personal data, never track you, and never send anything to external servers. Your audio and text stay on your computer only.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The BookCreator Audio Downloader Chrome Extension operates entirely locally within your browser. We do not collect, transmit, store, or process any of the following:

  • Personally identifiable information (names, emails, passwords, etc.)
  • Browsing history or analytics outside of the BookCreator application
  • IP addresses or location data
  • Telemetery or usage statistics

2. How the Extension Uses Data

In order to function, the extension interacts with specific "Website Content" (audio streams and text) only when you actively trigger it on app.bookcreator.com.

  • Audio URLs: The extension temporarily intercepts Firebase Storage URLs to allow you to download the audio to your local device.
  • Text Extraction: The extension reads the visible text on the page to generate a downloadable text transcript.

All of this processing happens strictly in your browser's local memory (RAM). Once the browser is closed or the tab is refreshed, this temporary data is destroyed. No data is ever sent to external servers or third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

The extension interacts directly with Google's Firebase Storage (which hosts BookCreator's audio). When you download an audio file, your browser requests the file directly from Google's servers. The extension itself does not route your traffic through any intermediate servers or proxies.

4. Permissions Justification

The extension requires specific permissions to function. Here is why we need them:

  • activeTab & tabs: Used to inject the automation scripts into your active BookCreator tab.
  • webRequest: Used strictly to intercept the audio URLs from BookCreator so they can be downloaded.
  • downloads: Required to save the final WAV or text files directly to your hard drive.

5. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, or if you would like to inspect the open-source code for this extension, please reach out via email at support@subhan.tech or visit the GitHub Repository.
